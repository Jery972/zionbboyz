WHO WE ARE

Zion Bboyz is a group of urban artists who have been working on socio-cultural initiatives around street art and breakdancing for over ten years. During this time, the association has organized events and created performances.

WHAT WE DO

We are dedicated to enriching the urban dance scene by offering a variety of activities. We provide learning and development opportunities through workshops, urban culture reflection events, as well as performances that showcase our creativity and art.

DANCE CONFERENCE

A captivating and dynamic event where our dance group combines performance and interaction with the audience on the art of breaking.

ARTISTIC CREATION

We push the boundaries of artistic expression by blending contemporary and hip-hop styles to create powerful and original performances.

SHOW

Our "Street Show" brings the vibrant energy of our dance group directly to the audience.

WORKSHOP

We offer practical workshops, creative exercises, and feedback through our workshops to help dancers of all levels refine their skills.
